The Southeast Asia Public Policy Institute supports the development of solutions to the most pressing public policy challenges facing Southeast Asia in the 21st century through research and dialogue.

About Us
What We Do

The Institute undertakes in-depth research to develop actionable policy solutions to the region’s most pressing challenges.

We convene dialogues with stakeholders and decision-makers to drive discussion on the policy challenges and opportunities across the region.
